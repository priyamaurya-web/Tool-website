Privacy Policy

Your privacy is our top priority

Last Updated: December 2024

At BgRemoverFree, we are deeply committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, protect, and handle your information when you use our free online background removal service. By using BgRemoverFree, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

Image Privacy and Data Handling

Uploaded Images

When you upload an image to BgRemoverFree for background removal processing, we take your privacy extremely seriously:

  • Temporary Processing Only Your images are processed in real-time and stored temporarily only for the duration of the background removal process, typically less than 30 seconds.
  • Automatic Deletion All uploaded images and processed results are automatically and permanently deleted from our servers within 1 hour of upload, regardless of whether you download the result or not.
  • No Permanent Storage We do not maintain any database or archive of user-uploaded images. Once deleted, your images cannot be recovered by us or anyone else.
  • No Training or Analysis Your images are never used to train our AI models, analyzed for marketing purposes, or shared with third parties for any reason.

Data Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

SSL/TLS Encryption

All data transmitted between your browser and our servers is encrypted using industry-standard SSL/TLS protocols, protecting your images during upload and download.

Secure Server Infrastructure

Our servers are hosted in secure, certified data centers with strict access controls, regular security audits, and 24/7 monitoring to prevent unauthorized access.

Access Restrictions

Only essential automated systems have access to processing data, and no human personnel can view your uploaded images during or after processing.

Information We Collect

Non-Personal Information

We collect minimal, non-personally identifiable information to improve our service:

  • Usage Statistics: Number of images processed, processing times, and error rates (aggregated and anonymous)
  • Technical Information: Browser type, device type, operating system, and screen resolution for optimization purposes
  • Performance Metrics: Page load times and service availability to ensure optimal performance

Personal Information (Optional)

We only collect personal information when you voluntarily provide it:

  • Contact Forms: Name and email address when you submit support requests or feedback
  • Email Communications: Information you provide when corresponding with us

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

BgRemoverFree uses minimal cookies and tracking technologies:

Essential Cookies

We use essential cookies required for the website to function properly, including session management and security features. These cookies do not track your personal information.

Analytics Cookies

We may use privacy-respecting analytics services to understand how visitors use our site. This data is anonymized and aggregated, containing no personally identifiable information. You can opt out of analytics tracking through your browser settings.

Third-Party Services

BgRemoverFree minimizes the use of third-party services. When necessary, we only work with trusted partners who maintain strict privacy standards:

  • Content Delivery Networks (CDN): For faster loading of website resources
  • Cloud Infrastructure: For reliable service hosting with automatic data deletion
  • Analytics Providers: For understanding aggregate usage patterns (opt-out available)

Important: We never share your uploaded images with any third-party service or partner.

GDPR Compliance

For users in the European Union, we comply with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). You have the following rights:

Right to Access Request information about what data we have about you (minimal as we don't store images)
Right to Deletion Request deletion of any personal data (images are already auto-deleted)
Right to Object Object to processing of your personal information
Right to Data Portability Request a copy of your data in a portable format

To exercise these rights, please contact us at privacy@bgremoverfree.com

Children's Privacy

BgRemoverFree does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately, and we will take steps to remove such information.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify users of any material changes by posting the updated policy on this page with a new "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.
